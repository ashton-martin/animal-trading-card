Dolphin

Known for their playful behavior, dolphins are highly intelligent. They are as smart as apes, and the evolution of their larger brains is surprisingly similar to humans.

  • Scientific Name: Delphinidae
  • Average Length: 4.2 - 26 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 6 years
  • Habitat: Temperate and tropical waters

Dolphins are a widely distributed and diverse group of aquatic mammals. Dolphins range in size from 5.6ft to 31ft. Although dolphins are widespread, most species prefer the warmer waters of the tropic zones.
